Meet Major

Major Prints is a Fort Smith-based 3D printing service built on a simple idea: fast, quality prints delivered to your door — no corporate overhead, just craftsmanship.

Whether you need a one-off prototype, a replacement part you can't find anywhere, or a custom piece that's uniquely yours, Major handles every order with personal attention from start to finish.

Local delivery preferred — Fort Smith and surrounding areas. Shipping available nationwide when needed.

// Common Questions

FAQ

We accept STL, OBJ, 3MF, and STEP files. If you don't have a file, describe what you need and we can discuss design assistance or recommend a designer.
Most orders are completed within 24–72 hours depending on size and complexity. Rush orders may be available — just ask when submitting your request.
Local delivery is preferred for Fort Smith and the surrounding area. We also ship nationwide — shipping costs are calculated based on size and destination and will be included in your quote.
We primarily print in PLA, PETG, and TPU in a wide range of colors. Specialty materials like ABS, ASA, or resin may be available depending on the project — mention your requirements in the order form.
Pricing is based on material usage, print time, and complexity. Submit your order request and you'll receive a detailed quote within 24 hours — no commitment required.
